About us

La lunar cafetaria is a pizza place where we have many customers and during lunch time or dinner time we have many clients and there also clients who are not able to displace themselves for a short time to come and get there pizza!

So we decide to create a web application in order to widen our customer base, where our customers order different sizes of pizzas with one or more toppings and what kind of crust they want.

Our customers decide if they want us to deliver the pizza or if they will come and get them. Here below there are instructions how our web application work!

Instructions to the customers:

In La Lunar's cafetaria, we have many things but this page is for pizza deliver and book!

  • Select the size of pizza which of course have the price on the side of the size you will select;
  • Also, you select what the kind of pizza do you want for example crips pizza, stuffed pizza or a gluten-free pizza;
  • And also what kind of toppings do you want on the pizza you want!
  • Choose if you want us to deliver or if you want to come and pick them.
